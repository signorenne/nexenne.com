#+TITLE: Disclaimer on the use of AI code assistants
#+OPTIONS: toc:nil num:nil ^:nil
#+STARTUP: showall

AI code assistants are allowed in this project. They can help explore ideas,
draft implementation details, and move faster through repetitive work.

The developer who submits a change remains responsible for every line they
commit, whether that line was written by hand, generated by an assistant,
copied from documentation, or adapted from another source. Review the output,
understand it, test it, and make sure it fits this repo before it lands.

AI suggestions must be checked for correctness, accessibility, security,
performance, maintainability, licensing, and alignment with
[[file:STYLE_GUIDE.org][STYLE_GUIDE.org]]. "The assistant wrote it" is not a valid
reason for introducing a bug or an unclear design.

Use AI as an assistant, not as autopilot. It can help with the work, but the
engineering judgement and final accountability stay with the person making the
change.
